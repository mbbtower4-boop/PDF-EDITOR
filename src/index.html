Quick guide

  • Highlight / Pen — drag on the page. With the Hand tool, click a highlight or stroke to select it: drag to move, ✕ or Delete to erase, corners resize a highlight. Everything bakes into the PDF when you save.
  • Text — click, type, then Enter. With the Hand tool you can drag it to move, drag a corner to resize, or double-click to re-edit the words. Delete key removes it. Text bakes into the PDF on save.
  • Image — drag an image file straight onto the page, or use the Image tool and drag a box. With the Hand tool you can then drag the picture to move it, or drag its corners to resize. Delete key removes a selected picture. Pictures bake into the PDF when you save.
  • Sign — draw a signature once, then click on the page to place it.
  • Pages — drag thumbnails to reorder, or use a thumbnail's ⇅ to jump it to any page number. Each has rotate & delete.
  • Insert PDF adds other PDFs at the beginning, end, or after a chosen page. Extract saves a page range.
  • Jump — type a page number in the bar at the bottom and press Enter.
  • Extract — select text right on the page (Hand tool) and Ctrl+C, or use the Extract tab to copy/save text and pull out embedded images (reuse them on the page or save as PNG).

Good to know

This tool adds text as overlays — it does not rewrite text already inside the PDF. "Sign" places a visible signature image; it is not a cryptographic digital signature.
