Quick guide

  • Highlight / Pen — drag on the page. Each has preset colors plus a rainbow chip for any custom color. With the Hand tool, click a highlight or stroke to select it: drag to move, ✕ or Delete to erase, corners resize a highlight.
  • Text — click, type, then Enter. To change words later: with the Text tool click the text and retype, or with the Hand tool double-click it. Hand tool also moves it and resizes by the corners.
  • Image — drag an image file straight onto the page, or use the Image tool and drag a box. With the Hand tool, drag to move or use the corners to resize.
  • Sign — draw a signature once, then click on the page to place it.
  • Pages — drag thumbnails to reorder, or use a thumbnail's ⇅ to jump it to any page number. Each has rotate & delete.
  • Insert PDF adds other PDFs at the beginning, end, or after a chosen page. Extract saves a page range.

Private by design

Your PDFs are opened, edited, and saved entirely in this browser — nothing is ever uploaded. This tool adds text as overlays; it does not rewrite text already inside the PDF. "Sign" places a visible signature image, not a cryptographic digital signature.
